About Us:

Who We Are & What We Do:

The Organization for Minorities in Technology (OMTECH), previously known as WITS, has been established to support and promote minorities in information technologies and encourage women and other minorities to study computer science and/or other information systems. OMTECH is dedicated to helping minorities gain self-confidence and "Computer confidence" by becoming involved in activities and projects that enable them to skillfully and comfortably use information technologies. This includes activities such as mentoring or role modeling, monitoring the status of women and other minorities in industrial and academic computing through guest speakers and company tours, and promoting communication among minorities in information technologies. While OMTECH is centered around supporting and promoting minorities, all are welcome to join!
