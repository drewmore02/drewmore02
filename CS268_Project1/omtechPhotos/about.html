All About US!

We are the organization for Women and Minorities in Technology. Our goal is to create an environment where students from all kinds of backgrounds can find a home with other students studying tech.

We love to spend time together and create lasting bonds. We meet every Thursday at 5:30pm in PHillips 265. Reach out to us using the information below if you are interested. Anybody can join and we would love to get to know you!
